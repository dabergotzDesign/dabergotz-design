ABOUT ME

Hello,

my name is Dennis Dabergotz, Game Developer with the focus on level design. Video games have a special meaning in my life and this exists until this very day. But what it means to develop a video game is something I learned at the School for Games.

Every Project I was involved with, I ended up to the position of a level designer. Now due to this experience and an ever growing love to level design, it is now something I really love to do and that’s where my passion goes in. Since I am a 90’s kid and I grown up with Nintendo games, which my orientation for the design comes from and, like Nintendo, the goal is to bring a smile to the face of every player who’s playing the games I was involved in. Born, raised and still living in Berlin, Germany, which I think Berlin is a very good place when it comes to game development.The dev community here is awesome, with a bunch of game studios, different events and game jams you’ll find a very good opportunity to meet with someone who has something to do as game developer. So, maybe we already met?

This should summed up my person and who I am, if not I’ll edit some things or better contact me if you want to know more about me.
See you.
