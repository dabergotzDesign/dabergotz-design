May I introduce myself

Hi everyone, my name is Dennis Dabergotz a Front End Web Developer with interest in design, games and technology.

Coming from a more hand crafting background, I turned my eyes on the digital way, which lead me to the School for Games to become Game Developer.

Graduating as Game Developer got me into Web Development through suggestions, because a career as Game Developer is fragile.
I felled more and more in love with Web Development. Especially the Front-End and Design part. "Hello UI/UX Design" I said to myself and now after some online courses & self-study on the material, I want to go the way as UI/UX Designer.
