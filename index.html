May I introduce myself

Hello everyone, I'm Dennis Dabergotz, a Front-End Web Developer with a passion for design, games, and technology. Originating from a hands-on crafting background, I transitioned to the digital realm at the School for Games, initially aiming to become a Game Developer.

However, upon graduation, I was steered towards Web Development due to its stability compared to game development. I quickly grew fond of Web Development, particularly the Front-End and Design aspects. This led me to explore UI/UX Design through online courses and self-study. Now, I'm keen to pursue a career as a UI/UX Designer.
